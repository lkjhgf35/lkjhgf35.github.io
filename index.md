Welcome!
Hey there! I’m so glad you stopped by the cabin for a visit! I’m Tanya, and I have a bit of a soft spot (okay, obsession) for turning everyday finds into something beautiful. If it can be thrifted, painted, stitched, glued, or completely reinvented… I’ve probably tried it—sometimes with a little chaos along the way.

This cozy corner is all about crafts that feel doable, fun, and just a little bit addictive. Think thrift store flips, handmade decor, seasonal projects, and gifts that feel extra special because you made them yourself. I love sharing ideas that don’t require a huge budget—just a bit of creativity and a willingness to experiment.

My goal? For you to leave the cabin feeling inspired, capable, and maybe even a tiny bit excited to start your next project. Let’s make something wonderful.
