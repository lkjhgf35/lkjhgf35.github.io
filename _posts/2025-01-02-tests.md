<h1>10 Free Felt Crafts to Try in My Cottage Craft Room</h1>

<p>
There is something about crafting with felt that just feels right in a sunny cottage craft room. 
Maybe it is the soft texture, the cheerful colors, or the way a few simple shapes can turn into 
something charming in an afternoon.
</p>

<p>
Since crafting is almost always happening around here, I thought I would share a few of my favorite 
free felt craft tutorials I have come across lately. If you have a few felt sheets, a needle and 
thread, and a quiet corner to sit in, you are already halfway there.
</p>

<p>Here are 10 free felt crafts to try.</p>


<h2>1. Felt Animal Ornaments</h2>

<p>
These sweet little animals are a perfect beginner felt project. Once you learn the basic technique, 
you can make almost any animal shape you like.
</p>

<p>
<a href="https://danslelakehouse.com/2021/12/how-to-make-felt-animal-ornaments.html">
https://danslelakehouse.com/2021/12/how-to-make-felt-animal-ornaments.html
</a>
</p>

<img src="https://danslelakehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/felt-animal-ornaments.jpg" width="500">

<p>
The idea is simple. Cut two felt shapes, stitch around the edges, add stuffing, and decorate with 
beads or embroidery. Even simple silhouettes like bears, owls, and seals turn out charming.
</p>
